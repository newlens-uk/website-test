Professional Credentials

Matthew Keys is a professional member of the British Computer Society (MBCS), the UK's Chartered Institute for IT. MBCS membership demonstrates commitment to professional standards, continuing development, and ethical practice in technology consulting.

The British Computer Society is the leading UK professional body for those working in IT, with over 60,000 members worldwide. MBCS sets and maintains standards for IT professionals, ensuring members stay current with industry best practices and professional ethics.
