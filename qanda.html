Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know about newlens managed automation services.

Getting Started

What exactly does newlens do?

We help UK SMEs automate repetitive work using intelligent workflows. Think of us as your automation partner - we set up your VPS (guide you through it - 5 minutes), build custom workflows, and manage everything ongoing so you can focus on growing your business. Unlike DIY platforms, we handle all the technical work. We also provide consulting and training services to help empower your business with practical AI literacy and strategic insights.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with newlens?

No! That's the point. We handle all the technical complexity. You just need to: get a VPS (we guide you - 5 minutes), explain your business processes (you're the expert there), review and approve what we build, and use the automations (simple interfaces, no coding). We translate business problems into technical solutions - you don't need to speak code.

How long does it take to get started?

Apply for alpha: Today
Discovery call: Within 48 hours
VPS setup: 5 minutes (we guide you)
We configure everything: 1-2 days
First automation live: 1-2 weeks

Our fastest: 5 days from application to first workflow running.

What size businesses do you work with?

We're built for UK SMEs - typically 10-100 employees across finance, legal, property, professional services, and healthcare. That said, we've helped solo consultants and also worked with divisions of larger enterprises. If you have repetitive processes eating your team's time, we can probably help.

Alpha Program

What is the Alpha Program?

We're accepting 10 UK SMEs to pilot newlens managed automation. You get 50% off for 3 months (£124-599/month depending on tier, plus your VPS at £10-20/month) and setup fees waived (save £1,500-3,000). After 3 months, you decide: continue at standard pricing, negotiate custom pricing, or part as friends. No lock-in - you own your VPS either way.

How do I qualify for alpha?

You need to be: UK-based SME (10-100 employees), have repetitive manual processes costing you time, willing to provide honest feedback and shape the product. Apply via the form, we'll schedule a 30-minute call to understand your needs. If it's a good fit, you're in!

What happens after the 3-month alpha period?

We review results together - did automations save time, reduce errors, deliver value? Then you choose: Option A: Continue at standard pricing (£249-1,199/month + VPS). Option B: Negotiate custom pricing based on usage. If you decide not to continue, the service simply turns off - you keep your VPS but the automation infrastructure is ours.

Why are you doing an alpha program?

We want to prove value before asking for full pricing. Alpha partners get discounts in exchange for: honest feedback on what works/doesn't, helping us refine the product, being case studies (if you're happy to share results). It's a partnership - we succeed when you succeed.

Technical & Implementation

What is self-hosted managed? Do I need to be technical?

Self-hosted managed means: You get a VPS from a cloud provider (£10-20/month, we guide you through signup - takes 5 minutes). We SSH in, install the automation platform, configure security, build your workflows, and manage everything ongoing. You never touch code or servers.

Why this model? Lower monthly cost (you're not paying us for hosting markup), data sovereignty (your VPS, your location, you choose where your data lives), transparent ownership (you own the VPS).

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Very likely, yes. We integrate with: Standard apps (Xero, HubSpot, Slack, Office 365, Google Workspace), custom APIs (if your system has an API, we can connect), legacy systems (screen scraping, file transfers when APIs don't exist), databases (direct connections when needed). Our platform supports 400+ pre-built integrations. During your assessment, we'll audit your tech stack and confirm compatibility.

What happens if an automation breaks?

We monitor 24/7 and catch most issues before you notice. Automated monitoring alerts us to failures, we diagnose root cause (API change? Data issue?), fix and deploy (usually within hours), then notify you of issue + resolution. Response times: Starter (48 hours, business hours), Core (24 hours, business hours), Growth (12 hours, business hours), Professional (4 hours, business hours). Plus, we build resilience into automations (retry logic, fallbacks, graceful failures). Most "breaks" are handled automatically.

Security & Compliance

Is our data safe? What about security?

Absolutely. Your VPS, your control: Data lives on your VPS (you choose location - UK options available), you have root access if needed. We secure it: SSL/TLS encryption, secure credential storage (encrypted vault), firewall configuration, regular security patches, SSH key authentication (no passwords). Monitoring: 24/7 uptime checks, automated alerts, daily backups. Self-hosted gives you maximum control - data never leaves your infrastructure.

Are you GDPR compliant?

Yes. UK-based company (data controller), your VPS can be UK data centre (you choose location during setup), Data Processing Agreements (DPA) provided, right to erasure/portability/access fully supported, privacy by design in all automations. We can also help you become more GDPR compliant - automations can enforce data retention policies, manage consent, and provide audit trails.

Who has access to our data?

Minimal, controlled access. Only newlens engineers working on your project, access logged and auditable, NDA and confidentiality agreements in place, access via SSH key (secure). We only access when working on your workflows - no standing 24/7 access.

Support & Service

What support do you provide?

All alpha partners get: Direct access to newlens support via email, response times based on tier (Starter 48 hours, business hours; Core 24 hours, business hours; Growth 12 hours, business hours; Professional 4 hours, business hours), monthly check-in calls (bi-weekly for Growth+), workflow monitoring & alerts, security updates, bug fixes (always free). We'll be implementing a support ticket system as we scale, but for now it's direct email communication.

What if we need changes to our automations?

Changes are expected! Your business evolves, automations should too. Included: Change time per tier (30 mins to 6 hours/month) covers small tweaks (adjust filters, change templates, add conditions). Bug fixes always free. Additional: Major changes (new workflow steps, different logic) - add extra change time at £75/hour (alpha pricing, £150/hour outside Alpha program), or upgrade to higher tier. We discuss costs before work starts - no surprises.

Can we manage automations ourselves eventually?

Yes - you have full control. You own your server and your data. We license the workflow IP to you as part of your subscription. Want full ownership? You can purchase the automation IP outright at any time and self-manage with our training support. Prefer managed? Most clients choose to stay with us - automations need monitoring, security patches, and optimization. We catch issues proactively so you don't have to. If you cancel: Your subscription ends with 1 month notice, automations are turned off, but you keep your server and data. No hostages, no lock-in.
